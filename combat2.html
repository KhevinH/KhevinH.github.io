Comparatives and Superlatives

Comparatives are used to compare two things. For most adjectives, add "-er" to make the comparative (e.g., "smaller"). If the adjective ends in "y," replace it with "ier" (e.g., "happier").

Superlatives are used to show the extreme of a group. For most adjectives, add "-est" to make the superlative (e.g., "biggest"). For adjectives ending in "y," replace it with "iest" (e.g., "happiest").
